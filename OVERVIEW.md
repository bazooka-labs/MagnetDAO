# MagnetDAO Overview

## What is MagnetDAO?

MagnetDAO is a founder-guided Algorand liquidity DAO built around a single core token, Magnet. Magnet powers governance, liquidity growth, and fee generation — the same token used for DAO voting also serves as the base asset in liquidity pools. The goal is to solve low liquidity on Algorand by deploying treasury capital and community participation to build deeper, more useful markets around Magnet and selected ecosystem projects.

## Mission

MagnetDAO's mission is to increase liquidity across the Algorand ecosystem while creating sustainable value for Magnet holders. By pairing treasury-backed liquidity with community governance, the DAO aims to improve trading efficiency, generate swap fees, and reinforce Magnet's value through real market utility rather than speculation alone.

## The Magnet Token

- **Ticker:** $U
- **ASA ID:** 3081853135
- **Total Supply:** 750,000 Magnet tokens
- **Distribution:** Tokens are distributed progressively as new liquidity pools are formed, tying supply growth directly to liquidity expansion rather than a one-time release.

Magnet serves as both the governance token and the liquidity anchor token for the DAO. Holding Magnet gives members voting rights and direct exposure to the fee-generating activity across all Magnet liquidity pools.

## Treasury Funding

The MagnetDAO treasury is funded through revenue generated by Bazooka Labs' applications. A portion of this revenue is allocated to the DAO treasury on a quarterly basis, providing a consistent and growing capital base for liquidity deployment. This model ties the DAO's financial health to the real-world performance of Bazooka Labs products, rather than relying on token sales or unsustainable emissions.

## Proposal System

Projects seeking liquidity support submit formal applications rather than being selected informally or through the influence of large holders alone. Each proposal must include:

- A description of the project
- The liquidity pair requested
- The amount of capital needed
- Expected market impact
- Timeline
- Known risks

If no projects formally apply in a given quarter, two outcomes are possible: quarterly treasury funds roll over into the following quarter, or the Founder may manually nominate projects for community consideration. As a founder-led system, the Founder retains final approval authority over all liquidity decisions to safeguard the token's value and the DAO's long-term integrity.

## Voting & Governance

Voting follows a quarterly cycle aligned with treasury accumulation and liquidity deployment:

1. **Discussion Phase:** Submitted projects are discussed openly in the MagnetDAO Discord. The community can ask questions, raise concerns, and build consensus before any vote is held.
2. **Official Vote:** At the end of each quarter, eligible proposals go to an official vote. Voting is weighted at **1 Magnet = 1 Vote**.

The DAO is intentionally structured so that participants are incentivized to vote responsibly. Larger holders carry more influence but also bear more downside if they support low-quality or misaligned projects — creating a built-in accountability effect. Early in the DAO's development, founder oversight remains elevated to ensure safety and operational stability, while the formal proposal process keeps decisions transparent and auditable.

## Liquidity Deployment

Quarterly proposal winners receive treasury-backed liquidity deployment. The DAO uses treasury funds to acquire the selected project's token and pair it directly with Magnet in a liquidity pool. DEX selection remains flexible, allowing the DAO to choose the platform and fee tier best suited to each deployment's strategy and target market conditions.

## Member Benefits

Magnet holders benefit in two concrete ways:

1. **Governance rights** — vote each quarter on which projects receive liquidity support from the treasury.
2. **Fee exposure** — as liquidity providers, Magnet holders participate in the swap fees generated by Magnet pools. Deeper liquidity reduces slippage, increases trading volume, and reinforces Magnet's utility through sustained market activity.
